MPC Autofill by u/Chilli_Axe

MPC Autofill automates the process of printing cards through MakePlayingCards.com with images hosted on public Google Drives.

Type your desired card list into the text box and hit Submit. One card per line. Click Search Settings to change which drives are searched, and the order in which cards are shown. You can also toggle between fuzzy (forgiving) and precise (exact) search modes.

Search for tokens by prepending the query with t: — for example, t:goblin.

You can upload your order as a CSV, specifying quantities and the queries for card fronts and backs. Download the CSV template here. Or, you can re-upload an order XML for further editing.
